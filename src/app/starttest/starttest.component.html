Online Assessment Guidelines


  • There will be 3 levels. Level 1 carries one mark for each questions, level 2 carries 1.5marks and level 3 carries 2 marks.
  • Make sure that your laptop/Desktop is fully charged.
  • Avoid wearing headphones/earphones during exam.
  • Using mobile phones or any other communication devices is strictly not allowed.
  • Make sure you have a good and stable internet connection.

All the best!!
